Core Ideas

Exposure

Exposure controls how bright or dark your photo is. It’s shaped by shutter speed, aperture, and ISO.

Focus

Focus determines what is sharp. Use autofocus points carefully, or manual focus for tricky lighting.

Composition

Composition is how you arrange elements in the frame. Strong composition makes photos feel intentional.
