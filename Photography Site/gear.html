Camera

Phones work well. A dedicated camera gives more control and better performance in low light.

Lenses

A “50mm prime” is popular for portraits. A zoom lens is flexible for travel and events.

Useful accessories include a tripod, memory cards, a camera bag, and a simple cleaning kit.
