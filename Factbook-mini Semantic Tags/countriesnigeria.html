Country Profile

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Abuja
  • Region: West Africa (Africa)
  • Population: Over 220 million
  • Area: ~923,768 sq km

Geography

Nigeria is located in West Africa and borders Benin, Niger, Chad, and Cameroon, with a southern coastline along the Gulf of Guinea. Landscapes range from coastal plains and rainforests in the south to savannah and semi-arid regions in the north.

People and Society

Nigeria is the most populous country in Africa and has over 250 ethnic groups. English is the official language; major languages include Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo. Christianity and Islam are the main religions, alongside traditional beliefs.

Economy

Nigeria’s economy is supported by oil and gas, agriculture, telecommunications, and services. Oil exports are important, though there are ongoing efforts to diversify the economy into technology, manufacturing, and finance.
