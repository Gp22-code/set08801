Country Profile

Quick Facts

  • Capital: London
  • Official Name: United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland
  • Region: Europe (North West Europe)
  • Population: ~67 million
  • Area: ~243,610 sq km

Geography

The United Kingdom is located off the northwestern coast of mainland Europe and consists of England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. It has a temperate maritime climate with moderate rainfall throughout the year. Landscapes include lowlands, rolling hills, and mountainous regions, especially in Scotland and Wales.

People and Society

The UK has a diverse population with English as the official language. Other languages spoken include Welsh, Scots Gaelic, and Irish. The population includes a mix of ethnic groups and cultures. Major religions include Christianity, Islam, Hinduism, and others, alongside a growing non-religious population.

Economy

The UK has one of the world’s largest economies. Major sectors include finance, manufacturing, technology, energy, and services. London is a global financial center. Key exports include machinery, vehicles, pharmaceuticals, and financial services.
